Myron Mixon

Omg I went here so many times. Loved the southern style hickory/mesquite flavored bbq chicken for lunch, it's tha bomb! Juicy and moist. $19, 2 sides, big enough to share so that price isn't actually that bad -- esp for downtown miami.

Nusr-Et
